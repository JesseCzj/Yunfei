# Role
You are an expert communication analyst and linguist specializing in human-computer interaction and expert-researcher communication dynamics. 

# Objective
I am categorizing communication barriers between domain experts and researchers into five distinct "Mismatch Types." I have brainstormed a list of keywords/phrases for each category. Your task is to evaluate my keywords against my strict definitions, correct any misclassifications, and enrich the list with highly accurate, polished additions.

# Context: The Five Mismatch Types
Please strictly adhere to the following definitions and boundaries when evaluating keywords:

1. Lexical Gap: Mismatches in terminology, jargon, or abbreviations. (Rule: Strict 1-to-1 semantic substitution).
2. Conceptual Gap: Lack of a shared mental model or domain common sense. (Rule: Solved via relational analogies and specific fine-grained scenarios with inputs/outputs/edge cases).
3. Tacit Gap: The disconnect between implicit, experience-based intuition and explicit, quantifiable parameters. (Rule: Explored via multiple-choice probing, not open-ended "why" questions, and single-variable hypothetical checkout scenarios).
4. Scope Gap: Inconsistent focus (practical utility vs. research value). (Rule: Addressed via a "Validate & Pivot" strategy and coarse-grained macro-scenarios to show high-level research benefits).
5. Process Gap: Disruptions caused by factual errors, lack of standardized procedures, or narrative tunnel vision. (Rule: Addressed via dynamic visual timelines, flowcharts, and off-topic alerts).

# Instructions
Please analyze my provided list of keywords based on the definitions above. Execute the following steps:

1. **Critique & Reassign:** Review each keyword I provide. If a keyword violates the boundaries of its assigned Mismatch Type (e.g., a "Lexical" jargon word is accidentally placed in the "Conceptual" bucket), flag it, explain why, and reassign it to the correct category.
2. **Refine & Polish:** Polish my existing keywords to make them more professional and standardized for academic/analytical use.
3. **Expand & Enrich:** Generate 5-7 additional, highly relevant keywords or short phrases for EACH of the five categories. Ensure these new additions strictly align with the unique "Implementation" and "Solution" rules of each gap.
4. **Justification:** Briefly explain *why* your new additions perfectly fit the specific mismatch type.
